Key Features

Powerful touchless control powered by computer vision and speech recognition.

🖐

Cursor Control

Move your mouse pointer by simply pointing your index finger in front of the webcam.

👉

Click & Drag

Left click, right click, and drag-and-drop using intuitive finger pinch gestures.

🗣

Voice Commands

Open apps, control windows, take screenshots, and more using natural voice commands.

Navigate PowerPoint slides with three-finger swipes — no remote needed.

🔄

Two-finger vertical movement for precise, jitter-free scrolling in any application.

GUI Dashboard

How It Works

Real-time processing pipeline from camera to action.

1

Capture

Webcam captures live video at 30 FPS for real-time processing.

2

Detect

MediaPipe identifies 21 hand landmarks with sub-pixel accuracy.

3

Recognize

Gesture classifier analyzes finger positions and movements.

4

Execute

System performs the mapped action — click, scroll, launch, or navigate.
